Brenda's Lasagna Recipe

Description:

This is a great lasagna recipe for those who like lasagna!

It works perfectly for dinner and as leftovers.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 0.5 pound(s) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 0.5 pound(s) shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes.

  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high

    heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and

    garlic and simmer 5 minutes.

  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch

    pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.

  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbling.
